Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know. If you don't see your question, reach out to us directly.

What is Awaken Dallas 2026?
Awaken Dallas is a premier, full-day life and leadership transformation experience featuring 5 world-class speakers covering mindset, health, wealth, AI, spirituality, and purpose. It's designed to catalyze profound personal and professional breakthroughs in a single day.
When and where is the event?
May 30, 2026 at the Credit Union of Texas Event Center in Allen, Texas. Doors open at 8:00 AM, and the event runs from 9:00 AM to 8:00 PM.
What's the difference between ticket tiers?
Standard ($97) gives you full event access to all sessions, networking, and event materials. VIP ($297) includes everything in Standard plus priority front seating, meet & greet with speakers, digital recordings, VIP lounge access, and lifetime community access.
Is there a refund policy?
Yes. Full refunds are available up to 30 days before the event. Between 30 and 14 days, you can receive a 50% refund or transfer your ticket to someone else. Within 14 days of the event, tickets are non-refundable but fully transferable.
Can I bring my family?
Absolutely! Many of our sessions are designed for personal and family growth. Several of our speakers specialize in conscious parenting, relationships, and family dynamics. Group discounts are available for 3+ tickets — contact us for details.
Will the sessions be recorded?
Yes, all sessions will be professionally recorded. Premium and VIP ticket holders receive complimentary access to digital recordings within 7 days after the event. Standard ticket holders can purchase recordings separately.
Is food included?
Light refreshments and beverages are provided for all attendees throughout the day. Premium and VIP ticket holders enjoy an exclusive catered lunch session with networking opportunities. There are also numerous dining options near the venue.
How many seats are available?
We are limiting this experience to just 500 attendees to ensure an intimate, high-impact environment. Our previous events have sold out weeks in advance. We strongly recommend securing your spot early — once seats are gone, they're gone.
What should I wear?
Smart casual is recommended. Dress comfortably but with intention — you're stepping into a transformative experience. Many attendees find that dressing well enhances their mindset and the overall energy of the room.
How do I get the most out of this event?
Come with an open mind, a journal, and zero expectations. The most profound transformations happen when you surrender to the experience. Network boldly, take notes, and commit to implementing at least one insight within 24 hours of leaving.
